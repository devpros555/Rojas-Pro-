How We Work

Our roofing process is simple, transparent, and built to ensure long-lasting protection for your home or business.

Step 01

Free Roof Inspection

We begin with a full roof inspection to assess the condition and identify any leaks, damage, or wear. You’ll receive honest recommendations based on what we find.

Step 02

Detailed Estimate & Material Selection

After the inspection, we provide a clear, itemized estimate. We help you choose the right materials for your roof based on your budget and style preferences.

Step 03

Professional Installation

Our experienced crew installs your new roof with precision, using quality materials and proven techniques. We keep the site clean and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Step 04

Final Walkthrough & Warranty

Once installation is complete, we conduct a final inspection with you to ensure everything meets expectations. You receive full warranty coverage and peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about roofing services, materials, and maintenance.

1 How do I know if I need a new roof?

If your roof is over 20 years old, has missing or curling shingles, leaks, or visible sagging — it’s time to consider a replacement. We offer free inspections to help you decide.

2 How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 1–2 days, depending on the roof size, weather, and type of materials used.

3 Do you help with insurance claims for storm damage?

Yes, we work directly with your insurance company to help you navigate the claims process and get your roof restored as quickly as possible.

4 What types of roofing materials do you install?

We install asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and flat roofing systems. We’ll recommend the best fit based on your location, budget, and goals.

5 Do your roofing projects come with a warranty?

Yes! Our roofing work is backed by a workmanship warranty and materials warranty provided by the manufacturer. We stand by every roof we install.
